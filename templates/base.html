Did you know that there's a lot more to periods than it seems? The four stages of the menstrual cycle can drastically alter people's mood, energy levels, and appearance. Tracking your Rosey flow, we'll let you know when those changes occur during your menses, so that you're able to regain control over your life. Rosey Flow sends messages during the Menstruation, Follicular, Luteal, and Ovulation stages of your period, and reminds you regularly what hormonal fluctuations you'll likely go through, what you should eat, and how you should exersize to always be your best self.
