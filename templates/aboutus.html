About Us!

We're Diya, Kathy, and Sofia!
We developed this site because we saw a need for it in our own lives. It is often hard to distinguish heightened emotions from those we consider to be our natural emotions due to hormonal fluctuations throughout the cycle. Of course, no emotion should ever be cast aside as "just pms-ing", but there are differences in way we feel emotions during different phases of the menstruation cycle due to our natural disposition as women. Our goal is to support women through each phase of their cycle and provide them with comfort and a basic idea of how they might be feeling, as well as why. We also provide tips to help them make the most of each of the cycle phases.
